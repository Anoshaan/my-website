About

Building digital products that feel structurally sound and emotionally precise.

I'm Anoshaan — a Lead UI/UX Engineer working at the intersection of interface design, interaction systems, and motion architecture. I design and ship products end-to-end, from research and architecture through production code.

I started in interface design at the seam between visual craft and engineering — building production UI for early-stage products that needed both polish and rigor. Over the last several years I've led design systems, motion libraries, and product flows for fintech terminals, AI-agent platforms, spatial commerce, and SaaS analytics.

My work focuses on the difficult middle layer of a product — the part where research, system thinking, motion, and code all have to agree. That's where good products feel inevitable, and where bad ones quietly fall apart.

Outside of client work, I write about interface mechanics, run small experiments in motion and typography (see Craft), and contribute to open design systems.
